Kendal Mitchell
Game and Software Developer

I'm a web, game, and software developer who's able to find success both independently and with a team. I'm highly adaptable and consistently maintain level-headedness in the face of challenges. I strive to find creative, yet pragmatic solutions to problems. 

I want others to feel empowered through the experiences I create.

During my childhood, I was captivated by the dynamic blend of combat, music, and narratives across countless digital realms and comics. These immersive experiences sparked a deep passion within me and ultimately led me to pursue a career where I could combine my love for both technology and artistic expression, thus beginning my venture into the realm of software development.

Now, as a software developer, my primary aspiration is to craft experiences that evoke the same sense of wonder and excitement that those digital worlds and comics once instilled in me.

Feel free to explore some of my projects showcased on this platform as I relentlessly strive to transform these childhood dreams into tangible realities.

Outside of my professional pursuits, you might find me playing my violin, catching up on movies, or more recently, fencing!
